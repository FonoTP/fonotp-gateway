Audio Diagnostics

This page does not use the gateway. It only tests browser speaker output and live mic playback.

How To Use It

First click Play Test Tone and confirm you hear a clear beep. Then click Loop My Mic and confirm your own voice comes back through the browser. If both work, browser audio is fine and the gateway return path is the issue.
